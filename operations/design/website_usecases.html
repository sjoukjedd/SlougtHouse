How it works

From body to insight

VU-AMS captures physiological signals from a five-electrode system and streams them in real-time to iPhone, iPad, or Apple Watch via Bluetooth.

01 — ACQUIRE
🔌

Five-electrode system

The device sits between the collarbones, connected to four lead wires: solar plexus, lower left rib, nape of neck, and lower back. ECG is measured on the front. ICG current is injected through the back, sensed at the front.

32 kHz carrier 1 mA constant current Electrode contact monitoring
02 — STREAM
📡

Bluetooth to iOS

Data streams via BLE to the VU-AMS app on iPhone, iPad, or Apple Watch. Live waveforms display in real-time. Recordings are timestamped and stored locally for offline analysis.

BLE 5.0 iPhone · iPad · Watch Live ECG + ICG display
03 — ANALYSE
💻

Deep offline analysis

Recordings transfer to the VU-AMS desktop analysis platform (Java) for detailed signal processing: HRV analysis, ICG waveform segmentation, stroke volume, cardiac output, baroreflex sensitivity, and full stress metric computation.

HRV · SV · CO · BRS Export to CSV / report macOS · Windows

Clinical psychophysiology

The gold-standard platform for laboratory and ambulatory autonomic research. Simultaneous ECG and ICG recordings enable separation of sympathetic and parasympathetic influences on cardiac function — the core of stress science.

"Pre-ejection period and HRV as independent markers of sympathetic and parasympathetic activity during a social threat task."

Software platform

Real-time ECG and ICG waveforms on iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch. Live heart rate, electrode status, recording control, and session management. BLE connection to the device with automatic reconnection.
