What makes VU-AMS unique

The ICG moat — measuring what no other wearable can.

Impedance Cardiography (ICG) gives researchers direct access to cardiac output, stroke volume, pre-ejection period, and baroreflex sensitivity — haemodynamic parameters that cannot be derived from ECG, PPG, or any other wearable signal. VU-AMS is the only ambulatory device in the world that delivers this in a chest-worn form factor with a mobile app interface.

ICG — Cardiac Output Stroke Volume · BRS ECG 2-lead · HRV

New in this generation

Both autonomic branches. One recording session.

SCL / EDA NEW

Skin conductance level (tonic) and skin conductance response (phasic). Sympathetic nervous system activity — complements HRV for full autonomic profiling.

PPG / SpO₂ NEW

Red 660nm + IR 940nm photoplethysmography for heart rate cross-validation and continuous oxygen saturation monitoring.

Body Temperature NEW

Continuous skin temperature via TMP117 (±0.1°C, 0.0078°C resolution). Thermoregulatory context for autonomic stress responses.

9-DOF Motion NEW

Full 9-axis IMU (accel + gyro + magnetometer, ICM-20948) for absolute body orientation, activity classification and artefact rejection.

Analysis platform

From raw signal to publication-ready output.

The iOS acquisition app provides live signal quality feedback and online HRV analysis. VU-DAMS (Java desktop) delivers comprehensive offline processing: artefact rejection, ensemble averaging, full ICG parameter suite, and export to SPSS, MATLAB and R.
