**To:** Principal

## Bottom line first

The VU-AMS occupies a gap that no competitor fully covers. There is no other wearable on the market that combines ECG and ICG simultaneously in a chest-worn ambulatory device with a mobile app interface. The closest competitor — MindWare Mobile ICG — is a research laboratory system, not a wearable. That gap is the company's most defensible position.

## Key findings

**1. The ICG gap is real and defensible.**  
No other wearable captures ICG alongside ECG in an ambulatory form factor. This gives VU-AMS exclusive access to stroke volume, cardiac output, and baroreflex sensitivity in the field. These are not derivable from any competitor's current wearable signal set.

**2. The main threat is not replacement — it is irrelevance.**  
If the research community accepts PPG-derived cardiac output estimates (via pulse transit time) as "good enough," the ICG advantage shrinks. Vasquez should monitor the literature on PTT-based CO validation closely.

**3. Empatica is a complement, not a competitor.**  
Their EDA signal captures sympathetic nervous system activity that we do not measure. The Slow Horses sensor study (Study 001) should evaluate EDA seriously — if we can add it, we cover both autonomic branches (parasympathetic via HRV, sympathetic via EDA) in one device.

**4. Vivalink and Hexoskin are early warnings.**  
Both are building clinical validation records (publications, FDA clearance). If they add ICG modules, the competitive window tightens. Track their product roadmaps.

**5. We have no FDA clearance.**  
Hexoskin has it. Vivalink has it. For clinical research contracts in the US market this matters. Flag to principal: regulatory strategy is a gap.

---

## What this means for the next-gen device

- ICG must stay. It is the moat.
- EDA is the highest-value sensor addition (Study 001 should confirm this).
- Mobile app (iOS) is a genuine differentiator — no research competitor has it. Keep investing.
- The Java offline analysis platform gives us depth no consumer device offers. Protect it.
- Clinical validation and publication record are the medium-term priority after hardware.
